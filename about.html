My name is Tristan Gibson, and I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. I’m a first year undergraduate information technology student at the University of Missouri. Back in high school, I was enrolled in an early college program called P-TECH, which was also known as Pathways in Technology. This allowed any students in the program to take college classes for dual credit since my freshman year. I have studied programming for over four years and graduated with honors, Dean’s List, an associate degree in science, network administration, and security, and a U Certify certificate. However, I believe that there is more to learn about programming and all of the different languages, so I enrolled in the University of Missouri to expand my knowledge of programming through lessons from professionals and hoped to find and make friends with other students who might share the same interest in programming as me.
