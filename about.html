My name is Tristan Gibson, and I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. I’m a first-year undergraduate information technology student at the University of Missouri. Back in high school, I was enrolled in an early college program called P-TECH (Pathways in Technology). This allowed students to take college classes for dual credit since freshman year. I have studied programming for over four years and graduated with honors, Dean’s List, an associate degree in science, network administration, and security, and a UCertify certificate. However, I believe that there is more to learn about programming and all of the different languages, so I enrolled in the University of Missouri to expand my knowledge and connect with others who share my passion for programming.
